This website is a long-term project documenting various things related to my life, sometimes also
what I'm working on academically.

One thing I stick to quite often is learning the fundamentals really well. If the foundations are
weak, then you can't do the higher level stuff that well. You can check my "Fundamental's Guide to"
series in the blog in order to learn my journey to be as fundamentally sound as possible.

In an age of AI, I also want to become adept at writing. It is very easy to just go and generate a
bunch of slop from your standard ["BS Machine"](https://www.transformingsociety.co.uk/2023/02/10/chatgpt-the-worlds-largest-bullshit-machine/). 
However, writing papers and other relevant articles in graduate school and beyond is a necessary skill for
success, and so I aim to be in the upper echelons of authors in terms of readability. I've always struck by 
the fact that the best scientists are those who are the best communicators.
